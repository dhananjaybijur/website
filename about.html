About doors2ai

We exist because AI engineering
needs a serious curriculum.

doors2ai was created to provide structured, practical training for people who want to transition into modern software development and AI engineering.

Mission

Our goal is to help learners build real skills through structured progression, practical projects, and clear guidance from an experienced instructor.

Teaching philosophy

doors2ai is led by an industry practitioner with experience delivering enterprise software projects, AI systems, and technical training in professional and academic settings.

  • Industry delivery experience
  • AI and software systems background
  • Agile project delivery experience
  • Hands-on mentoring approach

How we differ from competitors

We emphasize understanding systems, not memorizing tools. Students learn how software and AI systems are built, connected, and delivered in practice.

  • Structured curriculum
  • Real projects
  • Production thinking
  • Beginner safe progression
  • Career focused skills
