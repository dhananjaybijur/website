About doors2ai

We exist because AI engineering
needs a serious curriculum.

Not tutorials. Not certification farms. A structured path from zero to production-grade AI systems — taught with honesty about what LLMs actually are and what they aren't.

Mission

doors2ai exists to make structured AI engineering education accessible to people with no prior background — and to produce graduates who can design, build, and defend real systems, not just complete tutorials.

Teaching philosophy

We correct misconceptions before building on them. Learners understand why before they learn how. Every concept is framed around production-safe behavior and real-world constraints.

  • Misconceptions addressed in Module B before any agent work begins
  • Typed state required throughout — no sloppy dict passing
  • Guardrails and stop conditions taught before autonomy is extended

How we differ from competitors

Tutorial platforms teach isolated tools. We teach system thinking: state, orchestration, retrieval, governance, and delivery as an integrated whole.

  • No isolated tool tutorials — every module connects to the full system
  • Governance and safety taught from day one, not bolted on at the end
  • Capstone requires a defensible, production-grade deliverable
  • Cohort learning — accountability and peer collaboration built in
