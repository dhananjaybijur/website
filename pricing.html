What's included

Everything you need to succeed.

Every doors2ai program includes instruction, materials, and structured accountability — not just video access.

Live instruction

Regular live sessions with Dan — not pre-recorded videos you'll never finish. Real-time Q&A and walkthroughs.

All programs end with a project that is reviewed and graded — not a quiz. You ship something real before you graduate.
